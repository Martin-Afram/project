What is a noun

A noun is a word that refers to a person, place, thing, event, substance, or quality. Nouns are one of the basic building blocks of language and are used to name and identify various entities in the world around us. They can be concrete (like "dog" or "city") or abstract (like "happiness" or "freedom"). Nouns can also be singular (referring to one) or plural (referring to more than one).

What is a science

Science is a systematic and evidence-based approach to understanding the natural world and the phenomena that occur within it. It involves the observation, experimentation, and analysis of data to formulate theories and explanations about how the universe works. Science encompasses various disciplines, including physics, chemistry, biology, astronomy, and many others, each focusing on specific aspects of the natural world. The scientific method is a fundamental process in science that involves making observations, forming hypotheses, conducting experiments, and drawing conclusions based on empirical evidence.
